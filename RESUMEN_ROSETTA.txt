FRONTENDS (Parsers - Codigo fuente a IR):
------------------------------------------
1. rosetta-cobol     - COBOL (legacy business)
2. rosetta-fortran   - FORTRAN 77/90/95/2003/2008 (cientifico)
3. rosetta-quickbasic- QuickBASIC/QBasic (MS-DOS era)
4. rosetta-lisp      - Lisp/Scheme (funcional)
5. rosetta-ml        - ML/OCaml/F# (tipado funcional)
6. rosetta-planner   - PLANNER 1969 (AI planning)
7. rosetta-ops5      - OPS5 1977 (production rules)
8. rosetta-krl       - KRL 1976 (knowledge representation)
9. rosetta-prolog    - Prolog (logica)

BACKEND (Generador de codigo):
------------------------------
- rosetta-codegen    - Genera Rust desde IR

CORE:
-----
- rosetta-core       - Tipos base, traits, errores
- rosetta-ir         - Intermediate Representation
- rosetta-validator  - Validacion de IR

HERRAMIENTAS:
-------------
- rosetta-cli        - Linea de comandos
- rosetta-gui        - Interfaz grafica (egui)
